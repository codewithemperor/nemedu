Canadian Visitor Visa

Canada Work Permit

The Canadian visitor visa allows you to travel to Canada to visit family and friends, explore the country or study less than six months. Depending where you are from, you may need to apply for a Temporary Resident Visa (TRV) or Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA).

Why should you retain an immigration consultant to assist with your Canadian Visitor Visa application?

As a licenced immigration consultant living in Canada, Lucas Nonnemacher has the required knowledge to put together the strongest application for visitor visa. He will guide you through the entire process, from an initial eligibility assessment to the moment your application is processed and finalized by the Canadian government. Lucas will make sure that you filled out all the required forms and submitted the correct documents to IRCC (Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada).

Should I apply for a Canadian Visitor Visa or a Study Permit?

It all depends on your plans in Canada. You can study in Canada for up to six months if you come as a visitor, but we highly recommend people to apply for a Study Permit before arrival if you plan to stay in the country for more than six months.

Can I extend my stay in Canada or restore my status as a visitor?

Yes, you can, and Maritimes Immigration can guide you through the application process.
